Kyaka II is a place of hope, resilience, and community.

Learn More About Us

Our Mission

Providing essential services such as healthcare, education, and shelter to over 123,000 refugees.

Empowering individuals and families to rebuild their lives with dignity and hope.

Fostering a supportive community that promotes self-reliance and sustainable development.

Our History

1984

Kyaka II Refugee Camp established

1990s

Expansion to accommodate refugees from Rwanda

2000s

Influx of refugees from Democratic Republic of Congo

2010s

Implementation of comprehensive education and healthcare programs

Present

Continued support for over 123,000 refugees

Meet the Team

Tafar Mabi

CTO and Web Developer

Tafar is the technological backbone of Kyaka II, ensuring our digital infrastructure supports our mission to empower refugees. With his innovative approach, he's revolutionizing how we connect with and assist our community.

Nelly

Education Coordinator

Nelly leads our comprehensive education programs, dedicated to providing quality learning opportunities for over 30,000 children in Kyaka II. Her passion for education is transforming lives and building a brighter future for our refugee community.

Kelly Mabilo

Healthcare Manager

Kelly oversees our vital healthcare services, managing over 100,000 medical consultations annually. Her expertise and compassion ensure that every refugee in Kyaka II has access to essential medical care, significantly improving the overall health and well-being of our community.
